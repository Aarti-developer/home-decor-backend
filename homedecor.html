POTENTIAL IN
INDIA

The emergence and growth of the home decor industry in India are unprecedented. Home decor market was valued at $616.6 billion in 2019 and is estimated to reach $838.6 billion by 2027, registering a CAGR of 3.9% from 2020 to 2027. With the rise of digitization among the young population and rise of income has increased the demand for luxurious homes and one of the key industries riding this tide is the home decor business.
